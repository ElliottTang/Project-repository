Go Ahead Singapore Buses

The Go-Ahead Group is a multi-modal passenger transport operator founded in 1987. Formerly a small bus operator in north-east England, the Go Ahead Group has since expanded and diversified into rail services, providing more than a billion journeys each year in the United Kingdom.

The company entered the Singapore bus scene when it bidded for and won the Loyang Bus Package.
