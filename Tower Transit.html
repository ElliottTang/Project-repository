Tower Transit Buses

Tower Transit is an operator of bus services in London and Singapore. In 2015, competing with some of the world’s biggest bus operators, Tower Transit was awarded the Singapore government’s first competitively-tendered bus operations contract.

It commenced operations on 29 May 2016 and it is a subsidiary of Transit Systems.
